Ingredients

  • 3 bowls steamed rice
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2-3 tablespoons gochujang
  • 3 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 green onion chopped
  • 1 tablespoon roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 sheet of gim

Steps

  1. Heat up wide, flat pan with vegetable oil
  2. Add the kimchi and stir fry for 1 minute over medium high heat
  3. Add rice, kimchi juice, wataer and gochujang. Stir all the ingredients together for about 7 minutes with a wooden spoon
  4. Add sesame oil and remove from the heat
  5. Garnish with sprinkled chopped green onion, roasted gim, and sesame seeds. Serve right away
